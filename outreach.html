Diversity and Outreach

Lasers, Lenses, & Light Day

Lasers, Lenses, & Light Day was an open house outreach event held in March 2023 attended by approximately 200 people. As outreach coordinator for the Duke Optical Student Chapter, I led the organization and facilitation of this event. This free event was especially for middle school students and families in the Durham community and consisted of over 20 optics experiments and demonstrations, lab tours, and light painting.

Middle School Workshops

I worked with science coordinator and teacher at a local Durham middle school to plan a series of optics related workshops for a group of 6th grade students. This was an amazing experience as we were able to work with the same group of students over the course of several weeks and develop lesson plans for the workshops based on the students own curiosities.

Duke Engineering Graduate Ambassadors

Duke EGA is a graduate student-run organization that pairs prospective engineering graduate students with current graduate student mentors. The goal of this organization is to assist those looking to apply to graduate schools navigate the application process and offer perspectives on general graduate student life.
